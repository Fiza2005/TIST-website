About Us

Toc H Institute of Science and Technology, established in 2002, located in the suburbs of Kochi, embellished with dynamic management, diligent and dedicated faculty, and promising cream of the student community, is today a name to reckon with among the comity of Engineering Colleges in Kerala, making giant strides in all engineering branches and management studies.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized Institution that develops professionals with integrity who excel in their chosen domain making a positive impact in industry, research, business, and society.

Our Mission

  • To provide the ambiance necessary to achieve professional and technological excellence at the global level.
  • To undertake collaborative research that fosters new ideas for sustainable development.
  • To instill in our graduates ethical values and empathy for the needs of society.
